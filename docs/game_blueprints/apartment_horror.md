# Apartment Horror Blueprint

## Core fantasy

The player is alone in a familiar apartment, but ordinary routines become increasingly wrong.

## Recommended locations

- hallway;
- kitchen;
- living room;
- bedroom;
- bathroom;
- balcony;
- stairwell.

## Recommended mechanics

- lights and switches;
- phone messages;
- door chain or peephole;
- radio or TV audio;
- simple item search;
- one locked room.

## Example objectives

1. Make tea.
2. Check the fuse box.
3. Read a message on the phone.
4. Look through the peephole.
5. Find the missing key.

## Example horror events

- The TV turns on by itself.
- A shadow passes behind the balcony curtain.
- The peephole shows an empty corridor after loud knocking.
- The bathroom light flickers.
- A familiar object appears in the wrong room.

## MVP scope

Four small rooms, one phone message, one door event, one lighting event.
